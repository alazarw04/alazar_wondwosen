FAQ

I usually charge: • $15 - 30$ for short-form edits (Reels, TikTok, Shorts) • $50 - 100$ for long-form videos • $15 - 25$ for design(thumbnails) However, every project is different. If you want an accurate quote, just DM me on Telegram with: - The type of video you need - The script (if available) - Your goals I’ll review it and send you the exact price.

Turnaround time depends on the style and length of the project. On average: • Short-form videos: 1–2 days • Long-form videos: 3–4 days • Design work (thumbnails, visuals): 1 day For larger or more complex projects, the timeline may vary. I’ll always confirm the delivery time before starting.

I offer revisions to make sure the final result matches your vision. I’ll keep refining the video until it feels right for you. Just note that revisions should stay within the original project scope so we can keep the workflow smooth and efficient.

  1. Send a DM on Telegram with the style you want and the video duration.
  2. I’ll confirm availability and provide the price.
  3. You send the footage, script, and any required materials through a file transfer service.
  4. I begin editing and keep you updated throughout the process.

  6. Final delivery 🚀
